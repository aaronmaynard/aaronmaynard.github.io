FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We've got answers. If you have some other questions, see our support center.

How do I make an order or buyback request?
Highlight and copy the ice / ore you wish to submit for your buyback from the detailed view in your hangar. Click the link and paste your ice / ore in the text field. Select "Show advanced options" in the lower left corner, and make sure the following are set:
  • Designation: Appraisal to obtain an id.
  • Pricing: Jita and Buy if you are making a purchase or Sell if you are submitting a buyback.
  • Price percentage: 96.00 for overhead costs.
  • Comment: If you want to leave a comment, that's up to you.
  • Compactize: Checked
Submit and copy the appraisal id (ei: "PP7MT4") in the description of the contract. If submitting a buyback, copy the Buy price and paste it in the "I will receive" section of the contract. If making an order, copy the Sell price and paste it in the "I will pay" section. Set the expiration to 4 weeks, and contract to Eagle Company (make sure you select the right one).
How long does it take for my buyback to complete?
Buyback requests are usually handled between 2-3 days, however it could be longer. Make sure you have set the expiration date to 4 weeks. If buyback orders are not submitted correctly, the contract may be canceled.
What is the maximum order amount for fuel blocks?
The current limit is 100,000 Helium Fuel Blocks per contract or 2 billion ISK; we can fulfill this within 24 hours. Orders may be delivered at the following locations:
  • Perimeter - Tranquility Trading Tower
  • Jita - Caldari Navy Assembly Plant
  • Amarr - Emperor Family Academy
  • Ashab - Port Royal - EVE-Mogul
  • Any Eagle Company station
How is the hauling service cost calculated?

The cost for the hauling service is equal to 30% of an individual cut of the mining operation. You can see the equation here.

If there are 7 miners on grid (including one booster), the total capsuleers in fleet would be 8. Lets say when the mining op is over the fleet collected a total of 150 million ISK worth of ice/ore Jita buy, we would divide 150 million by 8, which gets us 18,750,000‬. This would be the payment to everyone if the shares would be divided up equally, however we charge only 30% of this!

30% of 18,750,000‬ would be 5,625,000 ISK for our cost of service. That leaves 20,625,000‬ ISK for everyone else each!

Can I request a refund?
Unfortunately, not. We do not issue full or partial refunds for any reason.
